Overview

This Excel-based financial model provides a comprehensive three-statement forecast for a firm in the consumer goods sector. It includes a detailed Profit & Loss statement, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow statement, along with supporting schedules for fixed assets, financial liabilities, and equity. The model is designed to help users analyze different scenarios by adjusting key financial drivers.

Financial modeling is crucial for business planning, allowing firms to predict future performance based on historical data and assumptions. This project aims to build a robust three-statement model for a consumer goods company, enabling stakeholders to forecast financial outcomes under various scenarios, evaluate business decisions, and plan accordingly.

View the full project on Github.

Approach and Methodology

  1. Workings: Collected, cleaned, and processed historical data to prepare for analysis.
  2. Forecast P&L: Developed a four-year forecast of the Profit & Loss statement and conducted scenario analysis.
  3. Forecast Balance Sheet: Modeled key balance sheet items using financial ratios and percentage of revenue.
  4. Forecast Cash Flow: Built a forecast of the cash flow statement and integrated it with the balance sheet.
  5. Supporting Schedules:
    • Schedule 1: Fixed Asset Roll Forward
    • Schedule 2: Financial Liabilities
    • Schedule 3: Equity Schedule
