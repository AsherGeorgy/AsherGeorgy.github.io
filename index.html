My Journey into Tech

My journey into tech began when I sought to track EPL and NBA scores using Excel. I quickly discovered the power of macros to automate tasks, which sparked my interest in programming and technology. This led me to explore areas such as data management, data visualization, and web development.

Turning a Hobby into Passion

Over time, my hobby turned into a passion. I began creating automated spreadsheets for personal tasks such as time management, to-do lists, and goal tracking. While pursuing a career in finance, I realized the huge potential of combining finance and big data with technology. While finance was interesting, using technology to create financial models was truly exciting. This drove me to explore advanced Excel and eventually dive into Python, SQL, data visualization tools like Tableau and Power BI, and even web development (HTML/CSS).

Continuous Learning and Projects

Through hands-on projects, experimentation with generative AI, and continuous learning on platforms like W3Schools and freeCodeCamp, I’ve developed proficiency in Python, SQL, data visualization, and web development.

What I Do Now

I am committed to constantly working to improve my expertise in data-driven decision-making and quantitative analysis. Recently, I developed and launched my first app on Streamlit. I’m currently diving deeper into Machine Learning with Python and advanced statistics, including the Stanford CS229 course.
